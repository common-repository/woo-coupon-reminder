The plugin's user-friendly design helps manage coupons, sends reminder emails, and encourages customers to use coupons before expiration.

== Description ==

Coupon Reminder for WooCommerce is what you are looking for if you use coupons to give customers discounts and encourage them to buy products in your store, but maybe the customer doesn’t know that they have a coupon. Coreem – Coupon Reminder for WooCommerce – a coupon reminder for WooCommerce plugin helps you to solve this issue. It helps you send reminder emails to customers to remind them they have coupons to use, which encourages them to purchase items before the coupon expired.

### FEATURES

&#9658; **With Coreem – Coupon Reminder for WooCommerce, you can:**

**Create Email Templates of Coupon Reminder for WooCommerce**:

The plugin allows creating multiple email templates of coupon reminders for WooCommerce with different content.

   - **Create email templates**: The plugin allows to create various email templates of coupon reminder for WooCommerce
   to send at different times.
   - **Subject**: you are able to change the subject of the coupon reminder for WooCommerce email.
   - **Heading**: change the heading of the coupon reminder for the WooCommerce email.
   - **Content**: change the content of the reminder email with text, media and shortcodes.
   - **Shortcode**: the plugin provides shortcodes, allows you to display necessary information in the coupon reminder email.

**Shortcodes**:
   - **{coupon_des}**: Description of the coupon.
   - **{coupon_amount}**: value of the coupon.
   - **{coupon_code}**: the code of coupon that will be sent to your subscribers.
   - **{coupon_expiry}**: the expiry date of the coupon.
   - **{use_coupon_button}**: the use coupon appear in the email. Allow customers to go to your shop and use the coupon. You can change the text and the link of the Use coupon button.
   - **{viwcr_site_title}**: the title of your website.

**Send Coupon Reminder for WooCommerce Email**:

Once a day, the plugin will scan your coupon and schedule to send reminder emails with the email template you created. You can also scan manually by using “Scan coupons” button in “Scheduled email” section.

   - **Scan coupons**: the plugin will scan your WooCommerce coupons once a day. With coupons which have expiry date and specific allowed emails address, the plugin will schedule to send coupon reminder emails.

   - **Sending time**: you are to select a time to send with each email templates. For example 1 day before the expiry time, 12 hours before expiry time…
